About

Real Jab is a new events company which specialises in Caribbean themed events. Our mission is to create memorable moments for our customers. We aim to achieve this by ensuring customer satisfaction is at the core of all decisions made within the organisation. It is about the fine details and we pride ourselves in taking all aspects of customer satisfaction into consideration when planning our events.
